Frequently Asked Questions

What are the park's operating hours?

SmileLand Water Park is open from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM daily.

What are the ticket prices?

General admission is $30 for adults, $20 for children under 12, and free for kids under 3.

Are outside food and drinks allowed?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed, but we have multiple food courts available inside the park.

Is there a dress code?

Yes, proper swimwear is required to use the water slides and pools.

Are lockers available?

Yes, lockers are available for rent to keep your belongings safe.
